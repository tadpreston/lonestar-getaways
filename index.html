About

Hi, I'm Tad Preston.

I'm the founder of Lonestar Getaways and a proud advisor with Fora Travel. From my home base in Texas, I help busy travelers plan trips that feel effortless from the first email to the final flight home.

My specialties are family travel, cruises, and all-inclusive resorts — but my favorite trips are the ones built around the people taking them. I take time to understand how you travel, what matters most, and where you really want to spend your time once you arrive.

Through Fora's partnerships, I can offer perks and pricing you won't find on your own — at thousands of hotels, on every major cruise line, and across the world's top all-inclusive brands. Best of all, my planning services are complimentary.
