The Advisor Advantage

Why book a cruise with a travel advisor?

Booking a cruise on your own means leaving real value on the table. Here's what you get when you book through Lone Star Getaways.

Onboard credits, specialty dining, spa vouchers, and cabin upgrades that are simply not available when you book directly, on many of the world's top cruise lines.

I have access to the same fares you see online, and I monitor prices after you book. If a better deal comes along before you sail, I'll get it for you.

Concierge Service

From first inquiry to the day you return home, I'm your single point of contact. Shore excursions, dining reservations, travel insurance, airport transfers. I handle the details.

Cruise Lines

Find the right line for you

Every cruise line has a personality. I've organized them by market tier so you can quickly find the style that fits your travel vision and budget.

Budget / Value

Great choice for first-time cruisers and value-seekers looking for a fun, affordable vacation at sea.

Carnival Cruise Line

Budget / Value

America's most popular cruise line, known for fun, high-energy ships with non-stop entertainment, dining variety, and affordable pricing. With 27 ships, Carnival operates the largest fleet in its parent company and delivers a vacation packed with activities for all ages.

Destinations: Caribbean, Bahamas, Mexico, Alaska, Mediterranean, Europe, Transatlantic
Best for: First-time cruisers, families, middle-income groups seeking fun and value; households earning $60K–$110K

Margaritaville at Sea

Budget / Value · Lifestyle Brand

The only cruise line built around the Margaritaville lifestyle: signature cocktails, live entertainment, casual dining, and a laid-back tropical vibe. Perfect for short getaways and brand loyalists who want their vacation to start the moment they board.

Destinations: Bahamas, Caribbean (Nassau, Key West, Freeport, Aruba, Curaçao, Montego Bay, Grand Cayman). Homeports: Palm Beach, Tampa, Miami
Best for: Adults 30–60 seeking a casual, party-friendly short getaway; Margaritaville brand fans

Mainstream

The heart of the cruise industry: big ships, incredible amenities, and something for everyone. These lines offer tremendous value and variety.

Royal Caribbean

Mainstream · Mass Premium

World's largest cruise line by capacity, famous for industry firsts: surf simulators, rock-climbing walls, sky-diving simulators, and private island destinations. "Best Cruise Line Overall" 22 consecutive years, with 30+ ships sailing to 270+ destinations worldwide.

Destinations: Caribbean, Mediterranean, Alaska, Europe, Asia, Australia, South Pacific, worldwide
Best for: Families, active travelers, adventure seekers, younger demographics (25–55) who want entertainment-packed ships
Virtuoso Partner

Norwegian Cruise Line

Mainstream · Mass Premium

Pioneer of "Freestyle Cruising": no set dining times, no dress codes, flexible scheduling. Known for multiple specialty restaurants, Broadway-caliber entertainment, and generous beverage and dining packages.

Destinations: Caribbean, Bahamas, Bermuda, Alaska, Europe, Mediterranean, South America, Hawaii, Panama Canal
Best for: Families, couples, and groups ages 35–65 who value flexibility, variety, and generous included amenities
Virtuoso Partner

MSC Cruises

Mainstream · International Focus

World's third-largest cruise line and European market leader. Modern mega-ships, multicultural atmosphere, and the exclusive MSC Yacht Club "ship-within-a-ship" concept for guests who want a premium upgrade without changing ships.

Destinations: Caribbean, Mediterranean, Northern Europe, South America, South Africa, Middle East, Asia. 250+ destinations. First Alaska season 2026.
Best for: Multigenerational families, international travelers, multicultural couples seeking European flair at sea
Virtuoso Partner

Premium

A step up in service, cuisine, and experience. These lines offer refined travel without the full commitment to ultra-luxury pricing.

Princess Cruises

Premium

Made famous by The Love Boat, Princess offers immersive destination experiences, the innovative MedallionClass wearable technology, and one of the most extensive global route networks in the industry, with 345+ destinations across all seven continents.

Destinations: Caribbean, Alaska, Europe, Asia, Australia & New Zealand, Hawaii, Mexico, South America, across all 7 continents
Best for: Active couples and travelers 50+; multigenerational families wanting broad global reach with comfortable, refined surroundings
Virtuoso Partner

Holland America Line

Premium

A 150-year-old premium line celebrated for cultural enrichment, world-class live music programming, and Alaska expertise. Features the Culinary Arts Center (powered by America's Test Kitchen) and itineraries visiting 175 UNESCO World Heritage sites across 35 countries.

Destinations: Alaska (industry leader), Europe (Mediterranean, Northern Europe, British Isles), Caribbean, World Cruises
Best for: Adults 50+, music lovers, culinary enthusiasts, intellectually curious travelers, and world-cruise devotees
Virtuoso Partner

Disney Cruise Line

Premium · Family

Disney storytelling, character experiences, and world-class entertainment on beautifully designed ships, plus adult-only spaces that make it genuinely enjoyable for parents too. Private island destinations include Castaway Cay and the new Lighthouse Point in Eleuthera (opened 2024).

Destinations: Caribbean, Bahamas (Castaway Cay, Lighthouse Point), Mexican Riviera, Alaska, Hawaii, Europe, Transatlantic (8 ships)
Best for: Families with children, Disney fans of all ages; North American and European families who want premium, immersive family cruising
Virtuoso: $100 pp OBC on select sailings

Virgin Voyages

Premium · Adults Only · Rebellious Luxury

Adults-only (18+) cruise line disrupting the industry with hotel-like design, included specialty dining at 20+ restaurants (no buffets), wellness focus, and curated entertainment. No kids, no nickel-and-diming. Voted #1 Large Cruise Ship by Condé Nast Traveler and Travel + Leisure.

Destinations: Caribbean (PortMiami), Mediterranean, Europe, North America, Transatlantic, with 100+ destinations across 4 continents
Best for: Adults 18+ who vacation without kids; Gen X travelers (avg age 48–49) with HHI $120K+ who value food, design, and wellness
Virtuoso Partner

Upper Premium / Luxury

Elevated service, smaller ships, and deeper destination immersion, without yet crossing into the all-inclusive ultra-luxury tier.

Cunard

Upper Premium / Luxury · Heritage

Britain's most storied ocean liner brand with 180+ years of maritime heritage and the only line offering regular scheduled Transatlantic crossings between New York and Southampton. Famous for White Star Service, formal evenings, ballroom dancing, and world-class lecture series aboard its iconic Queens.

Destinations: Transatlantic (New York ↔ Southampton, unique scheduled service), Caribbean, Alaska, Europe, Africa, Asia, Australia, Full World Voyages
Best for: Well-traveled British, American, and international adults 50+ who value heritage, formality, and ocean liner tradition
Virtuoso Partner

Explora Journeys

Upper Premium / Luxury · MSC Group

MSC Group's new ultra-luxury brand (launched 2023) offers "slow luxury" ocean travel: all-suite accommodations, butler service, 9 restaurants, and a destination-immersive philosophy. Intimate ships with only 461 suites, expanding to 6 ships by 2028.

Destinations: Mediterranean, Caribbean, Red Sea, Northern Europe, Iceland, Greenland, South America, Pacific Coast & Hawaii, sailings 7–44 nights
Best for: Affluent first-time luxury cruisers and experienced luxury travelers seeking a European hotel-at-sea experience
Virtuoso: €300 suite credit + VIP embarkation

Ultra-Luxury

The pinnacle of ocean travel: all-inclusive pricing, butler service, intimate ships, and the most exclusive itineraries on earth. This is where booking with an advisor matters most.

Silversea

Ultra-Luxury · All-Inclusive

Pioneer of modern ultra-luxury cruising since 1994. All-inclusive with butler service in every suite: open bar, specialty dining, Wi-Fi, and shore excursions included. Also the leader in luxury expedition cruising, accessing Antarctica, Arctic, and the Galápagos. Part of Royal Caribbean Group since 2018.

Destinations: 800+ destinations on all 7 continents, the broadest reach of any cruise line. Europe, Asia, Americas, Antarctica, Arctic, Galápagos
Best for: Affluent travelers (HHI $250K+, avg age 59–65) who want true all-inclusive luxury, global destination depth, and expedition access
Virtuoso: Up to $600 shipboard credit per suite

Seabourn

Ultra-Luxury · Small Ship

Ultra-luxury small-ship line founded in 1986 that defined the high-service, intimate cruising model. All-inclusive with open bar, specialty dining, and Wi-Fi. Intimate ocean fleet (458–600 guests) plus a growing expedition division accessing Antarctica and Australia's Kimberley coast.

Destinations: Mediterranean, Alaska, Caribbean, Antarctica, Northern Europe, Asia, South Pacific, South America, Chilean Fjords, Kimberley Coast
Best for: Well-traveled adults 45+ (avg age ~60) from US, Canada, UK, Australia who seek personalized service and refined, low-key luxury
Virtuoso: $100 pp OBC + up to 40% savings

Regent Seven Seas Cruises

Ultra-Luxury · Most Inclusive

The most inclusive luxury cruise experience afloat: all-suite, all-balcony ships where virtually everything is included: business-class airfare, unlimited shore excursions, specialty dining, fine wines, Wi-Fi, gratuities, and pre-cruise hotel stay. New flagship Seven Seas Prestige debuts 2026.

Destinations: 550+ destinations across 5 continents: Europe, North America, Africa, Asia, Australia & New Zealand, South America
Best for: Ultra-high-net-worth couples and travelers who want zero nickel-and-diming and the most comprehensive inclusions in the industry
Virtuoso: Exclusive credits + free suite upgrade (2026)

Celebrity Cruises

Premium / Ultra-Luxury · Modern Luxury

Modern luxury cruise line celebrated for architecturally award-winning ships, Michelin-pedigreed culinary programs, and sophisticated cultural immersion. With 280+ destinations in 70+ countries, Celebrity bridges premium and luxury, delivering a genuinely upscale experience at accessible price points.

Destinations: Alaska, Caribbean, Mexico, Panama Canal, South America, Antarctica, Mediterranean, Northern Europe, Asia, Australia & New Zealand
Best for: Couples and empty nesters 35–65 who prioritize fine dining, design aesthetics, and cultural enrichment. Adult-oriented atmosphere.
Virtuoso: Specialty dinner for two + $75 boutique credit
